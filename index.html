Old MacDonald's Farm

A Simon-style barnyard of fun!

Old MacDonald had a farm, E, I, E, I,...oh no. It's Simon, Old MacDonald's nephew. Simon is just learning his animals and animal sounds. To be a good helper on the farm, he needs to know his animals. Can you help him by repeating the pattern and clicking on each of the animals in the same order that they make a sound?
